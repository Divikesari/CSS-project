About Us

Welcome to D & S Resin Art Studio — a world of vibrant creativity and timeless artistry. We specialize in unique resin-based artworks that are handcrafted with love and imagination. From custom-made coasters to personalized keepsakes, our creations are more than products — they're experiences.

Every piece is a reflection of our passion for color, design, and storytelling through resin. We believe in blending functionality with aesthetics to bring joy and inspiration into every space.

  • Handcrafted with love using premium quality resin.
  • Personalized designs for all occasions.
  • Eco-conscious materials and packaging.
  • One-of-a-kind gifts that last forever.
  • Dedicated customer service and satisfaction.
